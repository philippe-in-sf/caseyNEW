At Trinity Episcopal Church in Tulsa, Casey Cantwell serves as Director of Music and Choirmaster. His work supports the parish's worship life through choral leadership, organ repertoire, service planning, and the formation of a music program rooted in the Episcopal tradition.

The 2025-2026 music season includes Tuesday noonday concerts, recitals preceding Evensong, choral services, and special concerts. The schedule below gives visitors one place to see the public music life of the parish without making this artist site pretend to be the whole parish website.
